<objective>
## Goal
Fix three production issues: receipt parser misclassifying "Add on" (no hyphen) products, missing green highlight for ACH-shifted payroll rows, and missing sale date field in standalone ACA entry.

## Purpose
Sales entry accuracy — agents can't correct the parser's product classification manually for every sale, ACH sales need visual tracking in payroll, and ACA entries land in the wrong pay week without a sale date.

## Output
- Fixed parser correctly identifies "Add on" variants as addons
- Payroll rows for ACH sales show green highlight
- Standalone ACA form includes sale date picker
</objective>
