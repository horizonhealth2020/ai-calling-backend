<objective>
## Goal
Add two new typography token sizes (2xs at 10px, xs2 at 12px) and migrate 76 hardcoded fontSize instances to use them — standardizing the remaining non-exact values from v2.5's token migration.

## Purpose
Phase 52 migrated exact-match fontSize values but left 97 non-exact ones. fontSize:12 (59 instances) and fontSize:10 (17 instances) are common enough to warrant proper tokens. The remaining 21 instances (9, 15, 20) are too sparse and intentionally non-standard — leave as-is.

## Output
- Two new token sizes in typography.sizes
- 76 hardcoded fontSize values migrated to tokens
- 21 intentional exceptions documented
</objective>
