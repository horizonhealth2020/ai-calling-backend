## Key Design Decisions (Phase 57)

| Decision | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Hero metric | Total Premium + Sales count, driven by period selector (today/week/last week/30 days) |
| Stat cards | This Week Premium, Commission Owed Friday (arrears), Chargebacks (trending alert), Lead ROI |
| Leaderboard | Condensed tracker: Rank, Agent, Calls, Avg (m:ss), Sales, Premium, Cost/Sale, Quality dot (●) |

| Activity feed | CS + manager actions only. Excluded: payroll period/adjustment events |
| Phone layout | Hero stacks, stat cards 2x2, leaderboard scrollable, activity feed full width |
| Chargeback alert | Red border pulse when trending up vs last week |
